Is Bad Credit Ruining Your Life?

A great credit report is the key to living the life you desire. Negative items on your credit report and low credit scores

  • Make you a credit risk to lenders
  • Raise your interest rate on vehicle purchases
  • Affect the price you pay for automobile and homeowners insurance
  • Decrease the ability to get credit cards

At Empire Business Credit Solutions, we have a system designed to remove negative and inaccurate items from your credit report, such as:

  • Foreclosures
  • Student Loans
  • Late Payments
  • Bankruptcies
  • Collections
  • Judgements
  • Repossessions
  • Tax Liens
  • Charge-Offs
  • Public Records
  • Short Sales
  • Medical Bills
